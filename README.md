The objectives of the Job Application Portal are centered around building a reliable, secure, and interactive online platform.
The core objectives of the Job Application Portal project are as follows:
1.Main Objective:
To develop a responsive and user-friendly platform for both job seekers and recruiters.
•	To simplify and speed up the job application and recruitment process.
•	To centralize all hiring activities, making it easier to manage and track job applications.
•	To reduce the dependency on traditional, paper-based hiring methods.
•	To improve the overall efficiency of talent acquisition through automation and streamlined workflows.

2.Technical Objectives:
To build a secure web portal with intuitive UI/UX for profile creation, resume upload, and job applications.
•	To provide recruiters with administrative tools to manage job postings and review applications.
•	To integrate real-time notification and communication features for better user engagement.
•	To use SQL for effective data storage, retrieval, and management while ensuring data integrity and scalability.
•	To implement automated job matching by analyzing and extracting skills from uploaded resumes.
•	To ensure cross-device compatibility and responsiveness through proper frontend design and frameworks.
This portal not only facilitates smoother job applications but also acts as a centralized hub for recruitment and talent acquisition.
